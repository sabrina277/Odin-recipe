Princess Cake

Home

Description

Princess Cake, lauded as Sweden’s most iconic dessert, was created in the 1920s, by cookbook author and home economics expert Jenny Åkerström. While she served as an instructor to the three Princesses of Sweden

Ingredients

  • Sponge cake
  • Vanilla custard(pastry cream)
  • Raspberyy jam
  • Whipped cream
  • Marzipan topping

Steps

  1. Prepare the pastry cream: Complete this step first since you need to chill the cream for at least 3 hours.
  2. Bake the cake: Bake the cakes and cool thoroughly and slice into four thin layers
  3. Assemble the cake: Spread the layers with raspberry jam and pastry cream and assemble them on a cake plate.
  4. Finish your masterpiece: Prepare your whipped cream and marzipan and top your cake. Finally, dust the cake with powdered sugar and decorate it with fresh raspberries.
